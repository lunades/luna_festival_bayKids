Partner Organizations

Safe Passages

"Safe Passages disrupts the cycle of poverty by engaging youth and families to build and drive a continuum of services that supports student success and community development."

Chapter 510

"Chapter 510 is a made-in-Oakland youth writing, bookmaking & publishing center. We believe that writing is an act of liberation for urban youth."

Youth Uprising

"Youth Uprising's mission is to transform East Oakland into a healthy/economically robust community by developing the leadership of youth and young adults and improving the systems that impact them."

Harbor House

"Harbor House aims to provide a safe place for opportunities, resources, and relationships to be shared so that children, teens, and families may flourish."

EOYDC

"The mission of the East Oakland Youth Development Center is to nurture the holistic development of youth from kindergarten into adulthood so that they can live a life of prosperity, integrity, and purpose."

Youth Beat

"Youth beat provides free digital media training to kids from some of the Bay Area's lowest-income communities."

Habitot

"Habitot Children’s Museum provides hands-on arts and “maker” activities, STEM explorations, and cultural programs."

MOCHA

"The Museum of Children's Arts has various arts programs for children of all ages in and out-of-school."

Homies Empowerment

"Originally fostering youth-development and educational focused initiatives, Homies Empowerment has led to community support to help meet their urgent food and basic needs."

Oakland Leaf

"Every year, Oakland Leaf provides quality, culturally responsive, and free after-school programming for East Oakland students at high-need elementary and middle schools."

Boys and Girls Club

"Boys & Girls Clubs of Oakland (BGCO) provides programs and services tailored to the special needs of today’s youth and supplementary to the endeavors of families, our schools, and other community-based organizations."

OUSD Family Resource Center

"The Central Family Resource Center provides OUSD families health care insurance and CalFresh enrollment assistance, workshops for families, and capacity building resources for school sites."
